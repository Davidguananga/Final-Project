My Name

I am a graphic designer , illustrator , and photography . In the artistic sector, I have quite significant talents. My design aesthetic is soft, contemporary, and Gen Z; I always approach concepts in a very direct and unique way. My varied upbringing and experiences in Spain and New York, I think, have had a big impact on my style. In addition to being aesthetically pleasing, my designs aim to be inventive and thought-provoking. My desire to remain current with emerging trends enables me to approach every project I work on from a different angle. In addition, my dedication to timeliness and great organizational abilities ensure that I always do excellent work by the deadline. Furthermore, my strong communication skills allow me to effectively collaborate with brands and understand their vision, ensuring that my designs align with their goals. Additionally, I continuously seek inspiration from various industries and art forms, allowing me to bring fresh and innovative ideas to the table

Real Faces of NYC

This photography series captures the authentic essence of New York City through the faces of its people. From the bustling streets of Manhattan to the quiet corners of Brooklyn, I aimed to document the diverse and vibrant individuals that make this city come alive.

Visual Quote

The design for "Okay Motomami" by Rosalia, released in 2022, focuses on the motorcycle, leather, helmet, and caos aesthetics. The word Motomami is divided into aggressive and cute parts, with red, black, and white colors representing the aggressiveness and edginess of the motorcycle aesthetic while incorporating softness and fun. The design aims to embody Motomami's dynamic character, inspiring empowerment and originality by fusing two diametrically opposed aspects.

Real New York

New York City has always been an inspiration for photographers. At Real NY, I aim to showcase the city's individuality and unique personality. Through my pictures, I take you on a journey that explores every corner of New York. My photos are a reflection of the city's energy and experience, capturing its essence in a way that will transport you to a New York state of mind.
